Condensing Economiser

Boiler Flue Heat Recovery Retrofit

A condensing economiser recovers waste heat from boiler flue gases that would otherwise be lost to atmosphere, using it to pre-heat boiler feedwater. This reduction in fuel demand increases overall boiler efficiency by capturing both sensible and latent heat through condensation.

How It Works

  1. Install economiser downstream of the boiler's flue outlet
  2. Route flue gases through a heat exchanger where latent heat (from condensing water vapor) and sensible heat is captured
  3. Preheat incoming cold feedwater before it enters the boiler, meaning the boiler needs less fuel to reach operating temperature

Retrofit Burner Control System

Precision Combustion Management

Modern microprocessor-based burner control optimizes combustion air/fuel ratio and boiler firing patterns. Mechanical adjustments are replaced with precise electronic controls that match heat output more closely to real demand, reducing excess air and improving combustion efficiency.

How It Works

  1. Microprocessor continuously monitors combustion conditions
  2. Electronic controls adjust air/fuel ratio in real-time for optimal efficiency
  3. System modulates firing rate to match actual heat demand, eliminating cycling losses
  4. Reduces excess air and improves combustion completeness

Refrigeration Cabinet Doors

Strip Curtains & Door Retrofit

Retrofitting display cabinets with doors, strip curtains, or covers reduces ambient warm air infiltration into refrigerated spaces. This simple intervention dramatically cuts refrigeration load and compressor run time while maintaining product visibility and customer access.

How It Works

  1. Install glass doors or strip curtains on open-front refrigerated display cases
  2. Physical barrier prevents warm ambient air from mixing with cold refrigerated air
  3. Reduced heat load means refrigeration compressor runs less frequently
  4. Product temperature stability improves, extending shelf life

LED Lighting Retrofit Kits

Building Lighting Upgrade

Retrofitting existing fluorescent tubes with LED replacement lamps or retrofit kits that fit into existing fixtures. LED technology consumes up to 50% less energy than fluorescent lighting while offering similar or improved light levels, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance.

How It Works

  1. Remove existing fluorescent tubes and ballasts from fixtures
  2. Install LED retrofit kits or LED tube replacements designed to fit existing fixtures
  3. LED technology provides instant full brightness without warm-up time
  4. Dramatic reduction in energy consumption and heat generation
  5. 15-25x longer lifespan reduces maintenance and replacement costs
