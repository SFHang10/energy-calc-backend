UK Government-Backed Energy Efficiency with European Impact

The Energy Technology List (ETL) is one of the world's largest databases of energy-saving technology, featuring over 8,000 independently verified and accredited energy efficient products across 21 technology categories, backed by the UK Government's Department for Energy Security and Net Zero (DESNZ). While UK-based, ETL-listed products are internationally recognized and widely used across Europe, supporting the continent's transition to Net Zero.

What Makes the ETL Special

Free & Impartial

Completely free to use for both purchasers and manufacturers, with government-backed impartial verification and transparent criteria.

Stringent Assessment

All products undergo rigorous independent testing and must represent the top 25% most energy efficient products in their market class.

Always Current

Regularly updated and aligned with government best practice, with products that no longer meet criteria being removed.

Net Zero Support

Directly supports the UK's Net Zero journey and helps businesses save on running costs during the energy crisis.
