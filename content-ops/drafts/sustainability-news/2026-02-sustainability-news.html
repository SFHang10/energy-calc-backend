European Circular Economy & Green Technology Updates

February 2026 Edition | Published February 2, 2026

February 2026 highlights a shift from circular economy pilots toward a single‑market framework, alongside a sharper emphasis on climate adaptation finance and targeted national funding. The European Commission confirmed a Circular Economy Act for 2026 and is tracking progress on the December 2025 circular measures package, while EEA indicators show many circularity targets remain off‑track despite emissions progress. At national level, the Netherlands updated ISDE rules, Spain opened a €202.5M renewables and storage call, and the UK extended the Warm Home Discount for the rest of the decade.
