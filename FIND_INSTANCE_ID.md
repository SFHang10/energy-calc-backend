The **Instance ID** is created when your app is **installed on a Wix site**.

### Option 1: Check if App is Already Installed

1. In your app dashboard, click **"Manage"** in the left sidebar
2. Look for **"Installations"** or **"Installed Sites"**
3. If you see "Greenways Buildings" listed, click on it
4. The Instance ID should be displayed there

### Option 2: Install the App on Your Site

If the app isn't installed yet:

1. In your app dashboard, look for:

   - Or **"Publish to App Market"** button
   - Or **"Install on Site"** option
