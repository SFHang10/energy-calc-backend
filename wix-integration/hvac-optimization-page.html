HVAC System Upgrade Guide

When to Consider Upgrades

  • Age: Systems over 15 years old should be evaluated for replacement
  • Efficiency: SEER ratings below 13 indicate upgrade potential
  • Repair Costs: Annual repairs exceeding 50% of replacement cost
  • Comfort Issues: Uneven temperatures or humidity problems

High-Efficiency Technologies

  • Heat Pumps: 200-300% more efficient than traditional heating
  • Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF): Precise temperature control with 30-40% energy savings
  • Geothermal Systems: 400-600% efficiency using earth's stable temperature
  • Smart Thermostats: Learning algorithms that optimize automatically
