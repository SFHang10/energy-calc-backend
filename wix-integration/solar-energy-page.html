Solar Installation Guide

Pre-Installation Assessment

  • Roof Condition: Ensure your roof is in good condition and can support the weight of solar panels
  • Structural Analysis: Professional assessment of load-bearing capacity
  • Electrical System: Evaluation of existing electrical infrastructure and upgrade requirements
  • Permits and Regulations: Understanding local building codes and permit requirements

Installation Process

  1. Site Preparation: Roof cleaning, safety equipment setup, and material delivery
  2. Mounting System: Installation of rails and mounting hardware
  3. Panel Installation: Secure mounting of solar panels with proper spacing and orientation
  4. Electrical Work: Wiring, inverter installation, and connection to electrical panel
  5. Testing and Commissioning: System testing, performance verification, and utility connection

Maintenance and Monitoring

Regular Maintenance

  • Visual inspection of panels and mounting
  • Cleaning panels every 3-6 months
  • Checking electrical connections
  • Monitoring system performance

Performance Monitoring

  • Real-time energy production tracking
  • Efficiency analysis and reporting
  • Fault detection and alerts
  • Historical performance data

Warranty and Support

  • Panel warranty: 25 years
  • Inverter warranty: 10-15 years
  • Installation warranty: 5-10 years
  • Performance guarantee: 25 years
