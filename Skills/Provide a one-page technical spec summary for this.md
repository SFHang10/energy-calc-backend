## Product overview

High‑efficiency converter‑fed asynchronous IE4 induction motor for use with an external variable speed drive (VSD), intended for industrial and commercial drive applications such as pumps, fans and conveyors.[^10]

***

## Identification

- Product name: Asynchronous IE4 motor (230V)[^10]
- Model number: 80SQ/4[^10]
- Manufacturer: NORD Gear Ltd, Abingdon, Oxfordshire, UK[^10]
- Listing scheme: UK Energy Technology List – Technology group “Motors, Drives \& Fans”; subtechnology “Converter‑fed motors (sold without VSD)”[^10]
- Date added to ETL: 18 September 2025[^10]

***

## Electrical and performance data

- Motor type: Asynchronous (induction) motor (classified as “Other – Asynchronous motor”)[^10]
- Efficiency class: IE4 (high‑efficiency, converter‑fed)[^10]
- Rated power output: 0.55 kW[^10]
- Number of poles: 4[^10]
- Rated operating voltage: 230 V[^10]
- Supply input: Multi‑phase[^10]
- Designed operation: With electronic variable speed drive (VSD) – converter‑fed motor[^10]
- Combined motor‑and‑drive efficiency: 84.1% (at test point)[^10]
- Test standard: Method 2‑1‑2A, Table 4, Section 7 of BS EN 60034‑2‑1:2014[^10]

- Technology category: Motors, Drives \& Fans[^10]
