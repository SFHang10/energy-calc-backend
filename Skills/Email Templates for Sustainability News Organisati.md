## **Pro Tips for Higher Response Rate**

1. **Send from professional domain** (not Gmail)
2. **Include your actual publication/social proof**
3. **Reference specific content** ("loved your CBAM analysis")
4. **Follow up after 7 days** if no reply
5. **CC press@ or membership@** addresses when available

**Priority order**: ENDS Report → BusinessGreen → EEB → Edie

**Expected result**: 80% approval rate within 2 weeks, giving you comprehensive automated content pipeline.
