# Key EU sustainability laws adopted in 2025 and timelines

Key EU sustainability laws saw significant developments in 2025, with several entering force, facing delays via the Omnibus package, or reaching critical implementation milestones.[^1][^4][^5]

## Laws entering force in 2025

| **Directive/Regulation** | **Adoption/Entry** | **Key obligations** | **Timeline** [^1][^3][^9] |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD)** | Entered force July 2024; transposition deadline July 2026 | Supply chain due diligence on human rights/environmental impacts for large firms (>€450M turnover) | Phased: 2027 (largest firms), 2028-2029 (rest) |
| **Eco-design for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR)** | Approved May 2024; applies 2025+ | Durability, repairability, Digital Product Passport (DPP) requirements | Delegated acts from 2026 (batteries/textiles first) |
| **European Green Bond Regulation (EUGBR)** | Full compliance deadline May 2025 | Green bonds must meet technical screening criteria | Ongoing from 2025 |
| **EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR)** | Originally Dec 2024/2025; **delayed** to Dec 2026 (small firms) | Due diligence on deforestation-free commodities (soy, beef, etc.) | Large operators: Dec 2025 (with 6mo grace); small: Dec 2026 |

## Critical 2025 implementation deadlines

**Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD)**:

- Large companies (ex-NFRD) reported FY2024 data in 2025 using ESRS standards[^5][^1]
- **Omnibus I "stop-the-clock"** (Apr 2025): Delayed Wave 2/3 CSRD reporting by 2 years[^4]

**EU Taxonomy**:

- Financial entities included Taxonomy alignment in 2024 non-financial statements; 2025 expanded DNSH/non-EU reporting[^1]


## 2025 simplification measures

**Omnibus I Package** (Feb 2025 proposal, Apr Parliament approval):

- CSRD/CS3D reporting deadlines delayed 2 years for mid-sized firms
- EUDR small operator deadline extended to Dec 2026[^6][^4]


## Looking to 2026+

| **Upcoming** | **Expected** | **Status** [^11][^12] |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Circular Economy Act** | Q3 2026 | Secondary raw materials market |
| **Energy Omnibus** | Q2 2026 | Simplified efficiency rules |
| **Green Claims Directive** | 2026 | Anti-greenwashing rules |

**2025 marked the shift from legislation to implementation**, with Omnibus delays providing breathing room while ESPR/DPP and CSRD Wave 1 drove immediate compliance across large EU firms.[^11][^4]
