# Could you provide me a list of 20 organisations in UK and Europe that would be good sources to get news a monthly news round of news in the sustainability sector and renewable energy sector and all things sustainable, recycling, circular economy related with links and additional info like groups to join etc

Here is a curated list of 20 reliable UK and Europe-based organisations for monthly sustainability news roundups, covering renewables, circular economy, recycling and climate action. Each includes their website, newsletter signup (where available), and groups/networks to join for updates and engagement.[^1][^2][^3][^5][^7][^9]

## EU-wide organisations

- **European Environmental Bureau (EEB)**
[eeb.org](https://eeb.org) – Weekly "News for a Greener Europe" newsletter; join as member org or subscribe.[^1]
- **European Climate Foundation**
[europeanclimate.org](https://europeanclimate.org) – Monthly insights on clean energy/transitions; partner network open to orgs.[^2]
- **ENDS Europe**
[endseurope.com](https://www.endseurope.com) – Daily EU environmental policy news; free daily alerts signup.[^3]
- **CONCORD Europe**
[concordeurope.org](https://concordeurope.org) – Development/sustainability reports; join working groups on financing/equality.[^4]
- **NewClimate Institute**
[newclimate.org](https://newclimate.org) – Climate policy briefings; CCPI index updates; research partnerships.[^6]


## UK organisations

- **ENDS Report**
[endsreport.com](https://www.endsreport.com) – Weekly UK environmental news/analysis; fines monitor + compliance calendar; free newsletters.[^7]
- **BusinessGreen**
[businessgreen.com](https://www.businessgreen.com) – Low-carbon business news; daily/weekly digests; events network.[^9]
- **Energy Technology List (ETL)**
[etl.energysecurity.gov.uk](https://etl.energysecurity.gov.uk) – Efficiency product updates; newsletter signup; manufacturer portal community.[^10][^11]
- **Edie**
[edie.net](https://www.edie.net) – UK sustainability news/events; free weekly bulletin; webinars/community forums.[^5]


## Renewables \& circular economy specialists

- **SolarPower Europe**
[solarpowereurope.org](https://www.solarpowereurope.org) – Solar market reports; member network (500+ companies).
- **WindEurope**
[windeurope.org](https://windeurope.org) – Wind energy policy/news; join events/working groups.
- **Circular Economy Coalition Europe**
[circulareconomycoalition.eu](https://circulareconomycoalition.eu) – Monthly circular updates; coalition membership.
- **Ellen MacArthur Foundation**
[ellenmacarthurfoundation.org](https://ellenmacarthurfoundation.org) – Circular economy news; learning community signup.
