# Please provide a news round up of sustainability news and programs for the whole of 2025 going into 2026

**2025 was a pivotal year for European sustainability**, marked by record funding through Horizon Europe and LIFE programmes alongside national circular economy strategies, with 2026 gearing up for legislative breakthroughs like the Circular Economy Act and Clean Industrial Deal.[^1][^2]

## Major EU programme milestones

- **Horizon Europe 2026-2027 Work Programme** (adopted Dec 2025): €14 billion total budget with €4.9 billion for climate action, including €540 million Clean Industrial Deal call for decarbonisation technologies and circular industry projects.[^1]
- **LIFE Programme 2025 awards**: €358 million funded 132 projects, 31 specifically advancing circular economy and quality-of-life initiatives across EU states.[^11]
- **Cohesion Policy circular investments**: €8.6 billion allocated via ERDF/Cohesion Fund for recycling infrastructure, SME circularity and waste prevention (2021-2027 frame).[^12]


## National highlights

**Netherlands**: Holland High Tech roadmap targets full circular economy by 2050, with focus on electronics refurbishment, AI scrap sorting and digital product passports.[^13]
**UK**: Energy Technology List (ETL) expanded listings like the NORD Gear 0.55 kW IE4 motor (Sep 2025), supporting retrofit schemes for high-efficiency motors, HVAC and refrigeration.[^14][^13]
**Spain**: España Circular 2030 strategy funded via EU projects for textiles recycling and industrial symbiosis; CLIC RECYCLE cross-border hair-to-biodegradables initiative.[^15]
**Portugal**: New Dec 2025 measures to accelerate circular business models across priority sectors.[^16]

## Key legislative advances into 2026

| **Initiative** | **Timeline** | **Focus** [^2][^4] |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Circular Economy Act** | Q3 2026 | Single market for secondary raw materials; design/repairability standards |
| **Clean Industrial Deal** | 2026 calls | €540M for low-carbon tech, circular materials, industrial competitiveness |
| **Energy Omnibus** | Q2 2026 | Simplified energy product rules, efficiency frameworks |
| **Ocean Act** | Q4 2026 | Marine habitat restoration, sustainable blue economy |
| **Climate Resilience Framework** | Q4 2026 | EU-wide adaptation, private investment barriers |

## Sector spotlights

**Circular bioeconomy**: CBE JU invested €2 billion in biorefineries converting bio-waste to high-value materials; top Horizon Europe theme.[^17]
**Buildings**: EU Missions (€246M Soil Mission, €220M Cities Mission) funded circular construction, deep renovation and urban circularity.[^1]
**Electronics**: EU-funded Sustronics projects pioneered bio-based, recyclable electronics; Netherlands led high-tech circularity events.[^18][^19]

**2026 outlook**: Expect acceleration via CSRD reporting deadlines, CBAM expansion and the legislative packages above, positioning Europe as circular economy leader while balancing competitiveness.[^2][^10]
