## üìù **Before Sending**

### **Required Customization**
Each template needs the following information added:

1. **Your Contact Information**:
   - Replace `[Your Name]` with your actual name
   - Replace `[Your Title]` with your actual title
   - Replace `[Your Email]` with your actual email
   - Replace `[Your Phone Number]` with your actual phone number

2. **Review Content**:
   - Verify product counts are accurate
   - Check product categories match
   - Ensure manufacturer name is correct
   - Review contact information

3. **Personalize**:
   - Add any specific details about your relationship with the manufacturer
   - Include any relevant statistics about your platform
   - Add any unique selling points

## üìß **Sending Instructions**

### **Email Format**
1. Copy the template content
2. Replace all `[Your Name]`, `[Your Email]`, etc. placeholders
3. Review and customize as needed
4. Use the subject line from the template
5. Send to the contact email listed in the template

- Set a reminder for 2 weeks if no response

- Be persistent but respectful
