Types of Water-Saving Faucets

30-50% Savings

Aerator Faucets

Mix air with water to maintain pressure while reducing flow. Easy to install, affordable upgrade for existing faucets.

40-60% Savings

Motion Sensor Faucets

Automatically turn on/off when hands are detected. Perfect for public restrooms and busy kitchens.

50-70% Savings

Touchless Smart Faucets

Advanced technology with precise control. Can be programmed for specific water amounts and temperatures.

25-40% Savings

Low-Flow Faucets

Designed with restricted flow rates. Simple, reliable, and cost-effective water conservation solution.
