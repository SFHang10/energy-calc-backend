Energy Technology List (ETL)

The UK’s government-backed register of independently verified, energy-efficient products — around 8,000 listings representing the top 25% most efficient in their class.

Official source. The ETL website opens in a new tab so you read their guidance in their own words. Greenways does not replace the register — our etl_* product rows are drawn from the same verified data.
