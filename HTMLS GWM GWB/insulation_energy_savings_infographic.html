Types of Insulation & R-Values

R-13 to R-21

Fiberglass Batts

Most common wall insulation. Easy to install, cost-effective, and provides good thermal resistance.

R-30 to R-60

Blown-In Cellulose

Eco-friendly option made from recycled paper. Great for attics and hard-to-reach areas.

R-20 to R-40

Spray Foam

Expands to fill gaps completely. Excellent air sealing and moisture resistance.

R-25 to R-45

Rigid Foam Board

High R-value per inch. Perfect for exterior walls and foundation insulation.
