⚡ UK Energy Technology List (ETL) — what is official vs Greenways

ETL official
What “ETL-listed” means (efficiency criteria)
UK Department for Energy Security & Net Zero (DESNZ), Energy Technology List — guidance for purchasers. Listed products meet published energy-performance criteria for their technology category; this is not a payback or cost estimate.
gov.uk/guidance/energy-technology-list · etl.energysecurity.gov.uk
ETL official
Heat recovery ventilation (HRV) as an ETL technology
ETL groups Heat recovery ventilation units separately from boilers, heat pumps, and general HVAC. Product eligibility and category names come from the ETL product database—not from Greenways payback or grant figures.
ETL product search
Greenways tool
Marketplace “See ETL example” links
Example product pages (e.g. heating equipment, HRV) are served by the Greenways marketplace, which maps ETL API product IDs to our catalogue. Specs and listing status are ETL-sourced; narrative payback, grant totals, and € ranges on the renovations page are not quoted from ETL unless explicitly cited to DESNZ/ETL guidance above.
