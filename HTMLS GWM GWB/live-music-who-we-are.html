Our story

We wanted playing live to feel simpler

We are a group of artists who know how hard it can be to find the right room on the right night — open mics, jams, sessions, and venues where you can actually get on stage and hone your skills.

So we built Live Music For Artists: a friendly home for discovering where to play, when sessions happen, and how to take the next step in your musical journey. Whether you are finding your first open mic or looking for a regular jazz jam, we are here to help you turn hopes and dreams into real gigs and real practice.

We have started with Amsterdam venues — building out jams, open mics, and sessions we know and can verify — and we are expanding to cities across the Netherlands, then further across Europe and beyond as the map grows with your help.

This site is for you — the player, the singer, the songwriter still looking for their people and their stage.

Grow the map together

Know more venues? We want to hear from you

If there is a jam night, open mic, or session we have not listed yet, please tell us. We review submissions and look to add trustworthy venues to the map — with schedules, links, and the details musicians actually need.

You can also use the Suggest a venue option on the jam map — we review every submission before it goes live. Or reach us through the contact page for performance videos, corrections, and ideas.
