🌊 Flood Prevention

Implement comprehensive flood protection measures:

  • Elevated utilities: Relocate electrical systems, HVAC, and water heaters above flood levels
  • Flood-resistant materials: Use concrete, ceramic tile, and marine-grade plywood in vulnerable areas
  • Smart drainage systems: Install French drains, permeable paving, and rain gardens
  • Flood barriers: Deploy temporary or permanent flood shields for doors and windows
  • Sump pumps: Battery-backup systems prevent basement flooding during power outages

💨 Wind & Storm Resistance

Strengthen structures against extreme weather:

  • Reinforced roofing: Impact-resistant shingles and hurricane straps reduce wind damage
  • Storm shutters: Protect windows from flying debris and reduce insurance costs by 20-40%
  • Structural bracing: Seismic retrofits and wind bracing strengthen load-bearing walls
  • Impact-resistant windows: Laminated glass withstands high winds and flying objects
  • Secure attachments: Metal connectors and anchors prevent roof uplift

🔥 Heat & Drought Adaptation

Prepare for temperature extremes and water scarcity:

  • Cool roofs: Reflective coatings reduce heat absorption by 50-60%
  • Thermal mass: Stone and concrete stabilize indoor temperatures naturally
  • Drought-resistant landscaping: Native plants require 70% less water than traditional lawns
  • Passive cooling: Strategic ventilation and shading reduce AC needs by 30-40%
  • Green walls & roofs: Vegetation provides natural cooling and stormwater management

❄️ Cold Weather Preparedness

Protect against freezing and extreme cold:

  • Pipe insulation: Prevent freeze damage and reduce heat loss by 80%
  • Air sealing: Eliminate drafts that increase heating costs by 20-30%
  • Heavy-duty insulation: R-40+ in attics and R-20+ in walls for extreme climates
  • Backup heating: Secondary systems ensure warmth during power outages
  • Snow load management: Reinforced roof structures handle heavy snow accumulation
