Why the Greenways Transition Agents Portal?

Transition Portal · sustainability journey

The Greenways Agent Transition Portal has been built to help businesses move through the energy transition with assistance and improved clarity on topics, products, and processes. As an organisation with a history of operating in business development and extended periods working alongside government organisations, we have developed a system of solutions that smooth the path for many businesses on their sustainability journey.

This matters especially for those in the restaurants and hospitality arena, where equipment, bills, and building upgrades all meet at once. The Transition Agents are your specialised guides in this space.

See energy use — then act on it

Saving energy starts with seeing it clearly. Greenways brings together monitoring views, utility breakdowns, and realistic visualisations so you can understand how your premises uses electricity, gas, and water — not abstract averages, but patterns you can respond to.

For operators who want an active picture of performance, the buildings dashboard and related tools show KPIs, trends, and drill-downs that help you spot waste, compare sites, and prioritise upgrades. The agents can walk you through what each view means and what to do next.

One service, many talents

The transition is not only about meters and charts. Businesses need to stay current on news and policy, find efficient products, uncover grants, subsidies, and government schemes, and compare finance and payback before committing to capex.

Rather than one generic chatbot, the portal offers a multi-talented team — seven named specialists, each with their own chat room in the interface. They hand off to one another when your question crosses topics, so you always talk to someone who knows that corner of the portal.

Portal tools the agents know

The Greenways website is a library of interactive portal tools that let you explore energy savings in novel, practical ways. The agents are grounded in that same catalogue inside this digital interface: they know what each tool does, when to use it, and how to get the best from it — ask in plain language instead of hunting menus.

  • Equipment intelligence & deep dive — compare baseline energy usage statistics against manufacturer standards to see if a piece of equipment is running inefficiently, then browse alternatives and grants on the same journey.
  • Product calculator & savings projections — model payback, grants, and upgrade paths with illustrative charts (do nothing vs upgrade, building vs equipment scope).
  • Schemes & finance finders — restaurant and EU portals, BNPL, green loans, and tariff context tied to real scheme rows.
  • Deals hub & sustainable product finders — live spotlights and lane-based search (water, electricity, gas).
  • News, map & media — monthly editions, case-study map, and video channels for staying up to date.
  • Buildings dashboard & sensor views — portfolio KPIs and monitoring entry points for ongoing ops.

Seven specialists — and growing

Each Transition Agent is a character with a dedicated chat in the portal (one agent per page — so conversations stay focused). They are not static brochures: their skill toolkits expand over time as we add schemes, products, tools, and data to the platform.

Think of them as a helping conduit on your sustainability journey — grounded in live Greenways portal content, honest about limits, and ready to point you to the right tool or colleague when a question needs another expert.
