How To Navigate The Map

On the map itself: mouse wheel or trackpad pinch zooms in and out. Click and drag on the map to pan around Amsterdam — separate from scrolling the page.

Also scroll the page up and down with the scroll bar on the right — independent of map zoom. →

You can also scroll the page using the venue list on the left — swipe or drag inside the list when it is long; the selected venue scrolls into view automatically.

When a venue is open, scroll inside its detail panel for schedule, links, and contact — the panel stays within the map and moves with pan/zoom.

Help is on the left and Reset View on the right (top centre) to return the map to its default size; use the ＋ / － controls at the bottom right to zoom step-by-step.

On mobile, swipe left and right to see the full map and venue list. Venue panels open centred on screen at eye level.

Pinch to zoom on the map, drag to pan, then use ＋ / － for step zoom. Help and Reset View stay visible at the top of the map while you scroll.

This helper appears once automatically — tap Help anytime to reopen it. Use − to shrink it; the chip reminds you about the scroll bar on the right.
