How To Navigate The Map

Use your mouse wheel or trackpad pinch to zoom in and out on the map. Click and drag on the map to pan around Amsterdam.

To scroll the whole page up and down, use the scroll bar on the right. You can also scroll using the venue list on the left — swipe or drag inside the list when it is long; the selected venue scrolls into view automatically.

When a venue is open, scroll inside its detail panel for schedule, links, and contact — the panel stays within the map and moves with pan/zoom.

Help is on the left and Reset View on the right (top centre) to return the map to its default size; use the ＋ / － controls at the bottom right to zoom step-by-step.

Tip: On mobile, swipe left and right on the screen to see the full map and venue list. Help and Reset View stay visible at the top of the map while you scroll.

Pinch to zoom on the map, drag to pan, then use ＋ / － for step zoom. This helper appears once automatically — tap Help anytime to reopen it.
